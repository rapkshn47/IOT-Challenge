Assignment 1

LM35 THermometer

Diagram

Components Required

  • Arduino Uno Board
  • LM35 Sensor
  • Breadboard *1
  • USB cable*1
  • Breadboard wire
  • LED * 6

Output

An experiment to show a simple thermometer. I used 6 LED's: 1 green, 2 yellow, 3 red. If the temperature is normal green will glow and if the temperature is little higher than the normal, then two yellow LEDs along with the green LED will glow. And finally when temperature is very high all the six LEDs will glow.
